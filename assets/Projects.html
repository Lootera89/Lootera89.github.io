GODS OF EGYPT: SECRETS OF THE LOST KINGDOM (2016)

Level Design VFX

For Gods of Egypt: Secrets of the Lost Kingdom, I designed gameplay waves and constructed the world using building blocks made by the artists. This iterative process included starting with large chunks and finishing with small decorative props like vases and foliage. I also took charge of atmospheric effects such as water splashes, dust, and clouds.

  • Level construction and decoration
  • Gameplay wave design and spawn routes
  • Camera curves
  • Creation of visual effects
